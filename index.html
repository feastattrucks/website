About us

Feast at Trucks strives towards branching out the food truck culture in India to various geographies and strives to bring a jump in the Food and Beverage scenario across the industry.


What we love

We love food. We also love the concept of food on wheels. Hence, Feast on Trucks!

What we do

We do amazing events in collaboration with food trucks of that particular area to bring all the food lovers at a single place. Marketing and branding?
Oh, yes!

What we believe in

We believe that Food trucks are a revolution in the Food and Beverage industry and should be present at all areas across the country

Season #2 Hyderabad Food Truck Festival

Welcome to The Season #2 of The Hyderabad Food Truck Festival.
What’s the best thing that The City of Nawabs is known for? The culture, people and the amazing varieties of food it has to offer. Drop in some great music and a wonderful flea market and you have India’s biggest Food Truck Festival in your hands. Start your spring right. Come and enjoy your Sunday at the heart of Hitec- City with more than 20 food trucks, live music and flea stalls from across the city. It is The “Sunday Funday” we all have been waiting for.
